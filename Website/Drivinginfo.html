Driving Test Information

Nervous about your driving test? We've got you covered!

Theory Test

The theory test assesses your knowledge of road rules and safety. It consists of multiple-choice questions and a hazard perception test.

  • How to Book: Visit the official DVSA website to book your theory test.
  • Practice Tests: Utilize online resources to practice theory test questions.
  • Study Materials: Consider using official DVSA publications or approved theory test revision courses.

Practical Test

The practical test assesses your driving skills and ability to follow road rules safely.

  • Booking Your Test: You can book your practical test online or by phone.
  • Test Format: The test includes a vehicle safety check, independent driving, and responding to examiner's instructions.
  • Show Me, Tell Me Questions: Be prepared to answer questions about vehicle safety features and checks.
